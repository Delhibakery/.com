OUR STORY

Delhi Bakery is more than just a bakery; it’s a celebration of flavor, tradition, and craftsmanship.

Established in the heart of Calgary, Delhi Bakery brings you a delightful fusion of classic Indian desserts and contemporary Western-style baked goods.

Whether you’re craving a creamy slice of cake, flaky pastries, or traditional sweets with a modern twist, our bakery has something to satisfy every palate.

Our Mission

At Delhi Bakery, our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality baked goods, combining traditional techniques with innovative flavors to create treats that everyone can enjoy.

We strive to create a welcoming space where every customer feels special, and each bite brings happiness.

With a passion for baking and a commitment to excellence, we aim to deliver a delicious and memorable experience with every visit.
